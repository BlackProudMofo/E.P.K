Blueprint // Profile

Honest Ornan Okoawo (Krexx Nv) is a Nigerian Afro-fusion artist who operates as an engineer of sound and a poet of "Naija Reality." His work deconstructs themes of survival, resilience, and spiritual friction with analytical precision.

Following a "digital death" that erased his early catalog, he rebuilt independently, founding Nimble Nova Entertainment. This act of "morphallaxis"—regeneration through reorganization—defines his current era of fierce creative control and unapologetic truth.
